Creative Scholars
African Summit 2023

The Global Art Exhibition brings together to community of artists, experts, academics and activists who comprise the creative commons affiliates network in different countries every 2 years. This year we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the commons, free culture and open knowledge. So if you are active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge-free software advocates, Wikipedians, Open knowledge, galleries, museums, archives, governemnt and foundations, lawyers and activists-we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about CC Art Exhibition 2023

ccsummit2023@cckorea.org

Creative Scholars African Summit 2023 Logo

The Creative Scholars African Summit 2023 Logo was designed using a combination of red and yellow and balck and white colors, inspired and symbolyzing creativity and art in simplicity. The logo features circles, symbolizing the program's value of creative and authentic thinking. The design was created by a team of skilled graphic designers who worked to capture the essence of the summit in a dynamic and visually appealing way.
